# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Evil Crow RF V2 project and its community will be treated with respect and dignity, regardless of age, body size, caste, disability, ethnicity, level of experience, family status, gender, gender identity and expression, marital status, military or veteran status, national origin, personal appearance, political affiliation, pregnancy status, race, religion, sex, sexual identity, sexual orientation, socioeconomic status, technology choices, or any other dimension of diversity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** - Using welcoming and inclusive language
- **Being constructive** - Offering and accepting criticism gracefully
- **Being collaborative** - Working together toward common goals
- **Acknowledging efforts** - Recognizing contributions and different perspectives
- **Being patient** - Understanding that people have different backgrounds and expertise
- **Protecting safety** - Not sharing personal information without consent

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination based on any protected characteristic
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Harassing photography or recording
- Sustained disruption of talks, events, or discussions
- Advocating for, or encouraging, any of the above behavior
- Sustained off-topic posts or personal attacks

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Project repositories and version control
- Project issue trackers and pull requests
- Project communication channels (Discord, GitHub, etc.)
- Community events and gatherings
- Official project websites and documentation
- Any space representing the Evil Crow RF V2 community

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting:

- **Joel Serna** - [@JoelSernaMoreno](https://twitter.com/JoelSernaMoreno)
- **Project Maintainers** - Via GitHub Issues (private message option)
- **Discord Moderators** - In our Discord community server

All reports will be kept confidential. The project maintainers are obligated to:

1. Acknowledge receipt of the report
2. Investigate the complaint thoroughly
3. Take appropriate action
4. Protect the privacy of the reporter

## Enforcement Guidelines

The project maintainers will follow these enforcement guidelines when responding to code of conduct violations:

### 1. Correction
**Community Impact:** Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome.

**Consequence:** A private, written warning and request to cease the behavior.

### 2. Warning
**Community Impact:** A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**Consequence:** A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No further interaction for a specified period, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct.

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Community Impact:** A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence:** Temporary ban from project spaces (1-3 months) with opportunity to appeal.

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Community Impact:** Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment, or aggression.

**Consequence:** Permanent ban from project spaces and community.
